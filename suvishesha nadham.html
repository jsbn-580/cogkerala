About the Board

Carrying the Voice of the Gospel

The Suvishesha Nadham Board of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in Kerala State is dedicated to publishing sound Biblical teaching and edifying content that strengthens the faith of the body of Christ.

Our seven-member editorial board oversees every aspect of the magazine ministry — from editorial direction to training writers and maintaining doctrinal and literary standards across every edition. We believe the printed and published word is a powerful vehicle for carrying the Gospel into every home.

With a commitment to both timeless Biblical teaching and contemporary storytelling, the board works to develop writers, coordinate special editions, and ensure that every issue reflects excellence and heartfelt devotion to the Lord.
