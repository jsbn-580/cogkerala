About the Board

Minister Excellence
& Accountability

The Credential Board of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in India, Kerala State, exists to maintain the highest standards of ministerial competence, spiritual integrity, and professional excellence.

Responsible for the examination, qualification, approval, and ongoing professional development of all ordained and licensed ministers, the Board ensures that every servant of Christ serving across Kerala meets rigorous standards of biblical knowledge, spiritual character, and pastoral competency.

Through regular training, ethical oversight, and compassionate accountability, the Credential Board protects the integrity of the ministry and equips pastors and evangelists to lead with wisdom, humility, and Christ-centred vision.
