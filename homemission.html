About the Board

Planting Churches,
Transforming Communities

The Home Mission Board of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in India, Kerala State, is dedicated to advancing God's Kingdom across our nation through strategic church planting and community transformation.

Comprised of 21 dedicated leaders representing all fourteen districts of Kerala, the Board coordinates evangelistic outreach, church-planting initiatives, and community service projects that serve as bridges to the Gospel. From pioneering missions in remote villages to urban church plants in growing cities, the Board ensures that Jesus Christ is proclaimed and the local church is strengthened in every locality.

With a vision that no community in Kerala shall be left without a vibrant witness to Jesus Christ, the Home Mission Board empowers local churches, trains evangelists, and deploys resources to reach the unreached and equip the reached for deeper discipleship.
