Our Foundation & Purpose

A Home Built on Grace —
Raising Children for His Kingdom

Mount Zion Children's Home, operating under the Church of God in India, Kerala State, stands as a beacon of hope for children in need across the region. Established with a mandate rooted in scriptural truth and compassionate action, the Home has provided shelter, education, healthcare, and spiritual nurture to hundreds of children across its years of ministry.

The Children's Home Board is the governing body responsible for the administration, financial stewardship, and spiritual oversight of all residential and outreach operations. Appointed by the Kerala State Executive of the Church of God in India, the Board members bring diverse pastoral experience, administrative depth, and a unified vision for child welfare ministry.

Our programmes span comprehensive residential care, formal and non-formal education support, vocational training for older residents, and active family reunification facilitation — all delivered within a faith-filled, disciplined, and loving environment modelled on the household of God.

The Board convenes regularly to evaluate welfare standards, approve allocations, monitor programme outcomes, and ensure that every child under our care grows in wisdom, stature, and favour with God and man — in the spirit of Luke 2:52.
