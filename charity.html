Honoring Lifelong Faith Securing Dignity for Our Pastors

We serve those who have spent their lifetimes serving the Kingdom. The Pastor Welfare & Charity Board establishes comprehensive retirement, medical assistance, and emergency relief programs across Kerala.

A Legacy of Benevolence

Pure Religion, Expressed through Active Support Compassion in Action

Sustaining those who have unreservedly laid down their lives to champion rural and urban missions across Kerala.

Our mandate spans across all fourteen geographical districts, responding proactively to unexpected medical crises, establishing micro-pension networks for veteran ministers who lack systemic financial fallbacks, and supporting education grants for families of active ministers.

Through robust administrative architecture, we process transparent allocations directly to validated recipients, creating an unbroken wall of security for our pastoral teams.
