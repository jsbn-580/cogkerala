About the Board

Breaking Barriers to the Gospel

The UPG Board of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in Kerala State is committed to identifying and reaching unreached people groups with the life-transforming message of Jesus Christ through culturally contextualized ministry.

Our six-member board focuses on research, strategy development, and direct ministry to communities that have limited access to the Gospel. We employ cross-cultural communication methods, partnership approaches, and compassionate engagement to build bridges to these communities.

With a vision that every unreached person in Kerala will have an opportunity to hear and respond to the Gospel, the UPG Board develops training programs, supports field workers, and coordinates strategic initiatives that bring spiritual transformation to marginalized communities.
