Message from Zion Hills

"Blessed be the Name of Our Lord Jesus Christ who commissioned us to be His disciples to make disciples."

Church of God in Kerala has come to a new era of challenges and with a vision to fulfill the great commission. We have to be visionaries and missionaries living to the call and proclaiming the love of our SAVIOUR to the lost, being HIS disciples.

Our small state Kerala has over 1200 local churches and 1500 plus ministers. 14 Revenue districts · 63 Taluks · 1479 Revenue villages · 6 Corporations · 87 Municipalities — a population of about 337 lakhs. Since A.D. 52 Kerala has heard the Gospel. It is vested on our shoulders to declare the good news and lead the lost to HIS kingdom.

Hence let us launch to FINISH Commission with great commitment as one body — fulfilling Matthew 28:19:

F — FIND the lost and disenfranchised
I — INTERCEDE and pray for a lost world
N — NETWORK with others to reach everyone
I — INVEST our resources strategically
S — SEND disciples to all nations
H — HARVEST a world of souls dying without Christ

Kerala State COG is an integral part of Church of God International — churches in 194 countries. Let us go forward to fulfill the commission with HIS power.

Church History

Church of God (Full Gospel) in India is part of The Church of God International — headquartered at Cleveland, Tennessee, USA. One of the world's oldest and largest Pentecostal denominations, present in 194 nations.

1886
R.G. Spurling founds the Church of God in Monroe County, Tennessee — the beginning of a global Pentecostal movement committed to Biblical holiness.
1907
Azusa Street revival expands Pentecostal fire worldwide. Church of God grows rapidly across continents under the leading of the Holy Spirit.
Early 1900s
Missionary Robert F. Cook establishes the Full Gospel Church in India. Kerala becomes a stronghold of Pentecostal faith and witness.
2025
102nd State Convention held in Kerala. Over 1,200 churches, 1,500+ ministers, and three Bible seminaries continue the FINISH Commission legacy.

Declaration

What We Believe

The Church of God stands for the whole Bible rightly divided. The New Testament is the only rule for government and discipline. Six pillars of our Pentecostal identity.

01
The Holy Bible

All 66 books — inspired, infallible Word of God. Supreme authority for all matters of faith, doctrine, and conduct.

02
The Holy Trinity

One God in three persons — Father, Son Jesus Christ, and Holy Spirit — co-equal, co-eternal, united in redemptive purpose.

03
Salvation by Grace

Salvation through repentance and faith in Jesus Christ alone. Redemption only through His atoning sacrifice at Calvary.

04
Baptism in Water

Believer's baptism by immersion — a burial and resurrection — in the name of Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

05
Baptism of the Holy Spirit

All believers are entitled to seek the Baptism of the Holy Spirit, evidenced by speaking in tongues as in Acts 2.

06
Healing & Second Coming

Divine healing through faith, and the glorious, imminent return of Jesus Christ for His church.
