Message from Zion Hills

"Blessed be the Name of Our Lord Jesus Christ who commissioned us to be His disciples to make disciples."

Church of God in Kerala has come to a new era of challenges and with a vision to fulfill the great commission. We have to be visionaries and missionaries living to the call and proclaiming the love of our SAVIOUR to the lost, being HIS disciples.

Our small state Kerala has over 1,200 local churches and 1,500+ ministers. 14 Revenue districts · 63 Taluks · 1,479 Revenue villages — a population of about 337 lakhs. Since A.D. 52 Kerala has heard the Gospel. It is vested on our shoulders to declare the good news and lead the lost to HIS kingdom.

Hence let us launch the FINISH Commission with great commitment as one body — fulfilling Matthew 28:19:

F — FIND the lost and disenfranchised
I — INTERCEDE and pray for a lost world
N — NETWORK with others to reach everyone
I — INVEST our resources strategically
S — SEND disciples to all nations
H — HARVEST a world of souls dying without Christ

Kerala State COG is an integral part of Church of God International — churches in 194 countries. Let us go forward to fulfill the commission with HIS power.

Rev. Y. Reji
State Overseer · COG Kerala · Zion Hills, Chengannur

Church History

Church of God (Full Gospel) in India is part of The Church of God International — headquartered at Cleveland, Tennessee, USA. One of the world's oldest and largest Pentecostal denominations, present in 194 nations.

1886
R.G. Spurling founds the Church of God in Monroe County, Tennessee — the beginning of a global Pentecostal movement committed to Biblical holiness.
1907
Azusa Street revival expands Pentecostal fire worldwide. Church of God grows rapidly across continents under the leading of the Holy Spirit.
Early 1900s
Missionary Robert F. Cook establishes the Full Gospel Church in India. Kerala becomes a stronghold of Pentecostal faith and witness.
2025
102nd State Convention held in Kerala. Over 1,200 churches, 1,500+ ministers, and three Bible seminaries continue the FINISH Commission legacy.

Declaration of Faith

What We Believe

The Church of God stands for the whole Bible rightly divided — six pillars of our Pentecostal identity that shape our worship, witness, and walk with God.

01
Pillar One
The Holy Bible

All 66 books — inspired, infallible Word of God. Supreme authority for all matters of faith and conduct.

2 Timothy 3:16
02
Pillar Two
The Holy Trinity

One God in three persons — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — co-equal, co-eternal, united in perfect redemptive purpose.

Matthew 28:19
03
Pillar Three
Salvation by Grace

Salvation through repentance and faith in Jesus Christ alone — redemption only through His precious atoning sacrifice.

Ephesians 2:8
04
Pillar Four
Baptism in Water

Believer's baptism by immersion — a burial and resurrection with Christ as commanded, an outward sign of inward transformation.

Romans 6:4
05
Pillar Five
Holy Spirit Baptism

All believers entitled to seek the Baptism of the Holy Spirit, evidenced by speaking in tongues as in Acts 2:4.

Acts 2:4
06
Pillar Six
Healing & Second Coming

Divine healing through faith, and the glorious, imminent, personal return of Jesus Christ — our blessed hope.

James 5:14

Ordained Servants

Ministry & Ordination

The Church of God recognises three tiers of ministerial calling — each representing different levels of training, credential, and Gospel responsibility.

01
Ordained Ministers
Fully credentialed clergy authorised to perform all ministerial functions — baptism, communion, and marriage rites
02
Licensed Ministers
Ministers in active training and service, working under supervision toward full ordination credentials
03
Exhorter Ministers
Entry-level ministerial credential recognising those called to preach and exhort within the local church fellowship
04
State Board
Governing the affairs of COG Kerala with prayerful accountability and strategic vision for the state
05
Planning Board
Strategic planning and vision casting for church growth, missions, and institutional development
06
Credential Board
Oversight of ministerial credentials, ordinations, and standards for all ministers in Kerala State
