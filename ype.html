Our Mission

Generation
Fire

The Youth Department of COG Kerala believes that the next great move of God will flow through the young. We create spaces for encounter, growth, and commission — equipping young men and women to be warriors for the Kingdom.

From youth camps and conferences to street evangelism and worship nights, our programmes ignite passion and purpose in the hearts of Kerala's next generation.
