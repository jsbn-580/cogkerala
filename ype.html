Our Mission
"
"Don't let anyone look down on you because you are young, but set an example in speech, in conduct, in love, in faith and in purity."
✦ 1 Timothy 4:12
The YPE (Young People's Endeavour) of Church of God Kerala is a movement raising up bold, Spirit-filled youth who live radically for Jesus. With active chapters in all 14 districts, organised across 4 zones, we equip young people through camps, conventions, talent programmes, sports events, outreach, and discipleship.

We believe this generation is called to be warriors of the faith — not spectators. Every programme, every camp, every competition is designed to produce disciples who will carry the Gospel of the Kingdom of God across Kerala.

Zonal Structure

Zone Level Competitions

All major YPE programmes operate across 4 zones — North, South, East, and Central Kerala — ensuring every youth has access to compete and grow at the zone level before advancing to the State stage.

Zone Talent Fest
Singing, drama, oratory, and art competitions at zone level — qualifying the best for the State Talent Test.
Zone Sports Meet
Football, volleyball, and athletics qualifying rounds — zone champions advance to the State Sports Championship.
Zone Bible Quiz
Scripture knowledge competitions at zone level — building Bible-grounded youth who advance to the State Finals.
Zone Revival Camp
Zone-level retreat camps for spiritual deepening before the State Annual Camp — prayer, fasting, and Spirit encounters.
Camps & Retreats

Transforming Camps

STATE CAMP
State Annual Youth Camp
The flagship event of YPE Kerala — thousands of young people converging at Zion Hills, Chengannur for days of Pentecostal fire, deep teaching, all-night prayer, and encounters with the Holy Spirit that leave no one unchanged. Lives are surrendered, callings are confirmed, and a generation is commissioned.
Prayer & Fasting Spirit Encounters Worship Nights Preaching Missions Call Testimonies
District Camps
Each of the 14 districts conducts its own annual camp — a preparatory ground for the State Camp, building local youth leadership and community.
Zone Retreat Camps
4 zone-level retreat camps connecting youth from multiple districts for cross-fellowship, spiritual growth, and zone-wide revival fire.
Leaders' Training Camp
Intensive leadership development retreat for YPE chapter leaders — equipping the equippers to build stronger local youth groups.
Missions & Outreach Camps
Specialised camps combining training with real field deployment — youth teams go into unreached villages directly from the camp grounds.
