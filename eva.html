Evangelism Department
"Go into all the world and preach the Gospel to all creation." — Mark 16:15
The Evangelism Department of COG Kerala is the frontline of the FINISH Commission. We deploy teams across all 14 districts, reaching unreached villages, conducting open-air meetings, literature distribution, and follow-up discipleship for new believers.
❤️
Charity & Relief
"The King will reply, 'Truly I tell you, whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers, you did for me.'" — Matthew 25:40
Charity & Relief ministry puts faith into action — feeding the hungry, clothing the needy, supporting widows and orphans, and extending Christ's compassion to the most vulnerable communities across Kerala.

Evangelism Activities

How We Reach Out

🎤
Open Air Meetings
Mass evangelism campaigns and crusades held in villages, towns, and public spaces — reaching thousands with the Gospel.
🏘️
Village Outreach Teams
Dedicated teams visiting unreached villages across Kerala's 1,479 revenue villages for door-to-door evangelism.
📰
Literature Distribution
Distributing Gospel tracts, New Testaments, and discipleship materials across every district in multiple languages.
🙏
Prayer & Healing Meetings
Spirit-led healing services and prayer campaigns proclaiming the full Gospel — signs and wonders confirming the Word.
📺
Media Evangelism
Using television, radio, and digital platforms to spread the Gospel beyond physical boundaries across Kerala and beyond.
🌱
New Church Planting
Identifying unreached communities and planting new Spirit-filled congregations — growing God's Kingdom across Kerala.
