Daily Practice

How to Pray

🌅
Morning Watch
Begin each day with 15–30 minutes of Scripture reading and personal prayer. "Early will I seek thee" (Psalm 63:1). Dedicate your morning before the busyness begins.
📿
Intercessory Prayer
Pray specifically for others — name by name, need by need. Stand in the gap for your family, church, nation, and those on this prayer wall who have trusted you with their burdens.
✝️
Pray the Scriptures
Take the Word and pray it back to God. "Let the words of my mouth… be acceptable in thy sight" (Psalm 19:14). Praying Scripture aligns your heart with God's will.
🕯️
Evening Reflection
Close the day in gratitude and surrender. "I will both lay me down in peace, and sleep: for thou, LORD, only makest me dwell in safety" (Psalm 4:8).
🤝
Prayer Partnerships
Find a prayer partner in your local church. Two in agreement carries extraordinary power. "Where two or three are gathered together in my name, there am I in the midst of them" (Matt 18:20).
🌍
Pray for the Nation
Lift Kerala, India, and the nations before God daily. "If my people, which are called by my name, shall humble themselves, and pray…" (2 Chronicles 7:14).
