About the Board

Rooted in Faith,
Driven by Purpose

The Church Growth and Mission Board of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in India, Kerala State, exists to fulfil the Great Commission — establishing, strengthening, and multiplying the local church across every community.

Constituted under the Kerala State leadership of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in India, the Growth and Mission Board coordinates all evangelistic endeavours, church-planting initiatives, and missionary deployment across the fourteen districts of Kerala. It serves as the strategic arm of the State, channelling resources, training, and vision into the field.

From pioneer missions in tribal and coastal belts to urban church-planting projects in growing cities, the Board partners with local congregations, district leaders, and international mission agencies to ensure that no community is left unreached with the transforming message of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.
