About the Board

Who We Are

The Believers Board is the supreme governing body of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in Kerala. Constituted by elected representatives from every district across the State, the Board provides spiritual oversight, doctrinal accountability, and administrative direction to the entire church fellowship.

Rooted in the Pentecostal heritage and the full gospel of Jesus Christ, Board members serve as trustees of the faith — protecting the integrity of the Church's mission, resources, and witness. Every decision is made prayerfully, with the Word of God as the final authority.

The Board meets regularly to review ministry reports, ratify appointments, approve budgets, and chart the course for evangelism, discipleship, and social outreach across all 14 districts of Kerala.

Schedule

Board Meetings

01
Annual General Meeting
The full Board convenes once a year at the State headquarters. All members present ministry reports, review accounts, approve next year's budget, and set the strategic direction for the Church across Kerala.
02
Quarterly Review Sessions
Executive committee meets every quarter to review progress on resolutions, address urgent church matters, and provide interim governance between the annual sessions.
03
District Consultations
Board members periodically visit their respective districts, holding consultations with local overseers, elders, and congregations to stay connected to the grassroots Church.
04
Special Convocations
Called as needed for urgent matters — ordinations, doctrinal clarifications, emergency appropriations, or responding to significant opportunities for the Kingdom of God in Kerala.
