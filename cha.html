Our Mission

Love in
Action

The Charity Department of COG Kerala operationalises the command of Scripture — to care for the orphan, the widow, the poor, and the forgotten. Our network of children's homes, relief programmes, and community care initiatives are the hands and feet of Jesus.

Every act of compassion is a proclamation of the Gospel. When we serve the least of these, we serve Christ Himself — and we invite Kerala to know the God who sees and cares.
