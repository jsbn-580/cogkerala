About the Ministry

Ink that carries Light

The Literature Ministry of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in Kerala State exists to spread the Gospel and deepen believers' faith through the printed and digital word.

We produce Bible-centered magazines, devotional booklets, gospel tracts, and Sunday School materials that are distributed through local churches, outreach programs, and subscription across Kerala.

Our editorial team works to ensure sound doctrine, cultural relevance, and readability, so that every piece of literature — whether for a new believer or a lifelong member — builds up the body of Christ.
