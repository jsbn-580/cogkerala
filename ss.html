The Sunday School Department of Church of God Kerala is committed to nurturing children and youth in Biblical knowledge and Pentecostal faith. With over 1,200 Sunday Schools across all 14 districts, we reach thousands of young hearts each week with the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Our curriculum is grounded in Scripture, Spirit-led, and age-appropriate — from LKG through Class 12. Each level has its own dedicated textbooks, activity books, and teacher guides, equipping every child and teacher for a fruitful ministry.

What We Do

Our Activities

01
Bible Quiz Competitions
State-level competitions testing Biblical knowledge — motivating students to memorise and meditate on Scripture across all 14 districts of Kerala.
02
Creative Arts & Crafts
Expressing faith through art, drawing, and craft activities rooted in Biblical stories and themes — nurturing creativity as an act of worship.
03
Children's Choir & Worship
Training children to lead in worship — developing a generation of Spirit-filled worshippers who glorify God through song, music, and praise.
04
Annual Summer Camps
Intensive spiritual retreats for children and youth — prayer, worship, deep teaching, and building lifelong bonds of faith and fellowship.
05
Drama & Biblical Presentations
Christmas programs, Easter pageants, and Biblical dramas — communicating the Gospel through creative performance and powerful storytelling.
06
Teacher Training Workshops
Equipping Sunday School teachers with modern pedagogical skills rooted in Biblical principles, Pentecostal theology, and child development.
