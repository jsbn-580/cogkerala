Our Mission

To Every
Nation

The Mission Board of COG Kerala coordinates the church's cross-cultural missionary endeavours — from tribal communities in Kerala's forests to diaspora Keralites serving the Gospel abroad. We are part of a 194-nation global movement.

Through strategic partnerships with COG International's World Mission department and the Asia Pacific regional office, Kerala's missionaries are planted in harvest fields that stretch far beyond state borders.

Home Missions
