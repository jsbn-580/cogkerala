What We Do

LM Activities

State and Zonal Camps
Annual Camps uniting thousands of women in intercession for families, communities, and Kerala.
United Prayer
Weekly and monthly prayer sessions in zonal,center levels.
Charity & Care Projects
Visiting hospitals, orphanages, and homes for the elderly — expressing Christ's compassion through hands-on service.
Leadership Development
Training seminars and workshops equipping women to lead prayer groups, teach Sunday School, and serve their communities.
Mobilising LM members to pray for and support missionaries — partnering with the Mission Board for global outreach.
Family Strengthening
Marriage enrichment retreats, parenting seminars, and family prayer programs — building strong, Spirit-filled homes in Kerala.
