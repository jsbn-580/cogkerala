About the Ministry

Women of Grace,
Women of God

The Ladies Ministry of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in Kerala brings together women from all walks of life — united in faith, prayer, and a passion for God's kingdom.

Across 1,200+ prayer groups in 14 districts, women gather weekly to pray, worship, and strengthen each other in Christ. From state-level camps to zonal prayer conventions and community charity work, the Ladies Ministry is at the heart of family life and church growth across Kerala.

We believe in empowering women to discover their calling, equipping them for leadership, and uniting them in a fellowship of grace that impacts families, communities, and the world.

🙏

What We Do

LM Activities

Through prayer, community service, and spiritual leadership, we serve the Kingdom across Kerala.
🏕️
State and Zonal Camps
Annual camps uniting thousands of women in intercession for families, communities, and all of Kerala.
🙏
United Prayer
Weekly and monthly prayer sessions at zonal and centre levels — interceding for the church and nation.
❤️
Charity & Care Projects
Visiting hospitals, orphanages, and homes for the elderly — expressing Christ's compassion through service.
🌟
Leadership Development
Training seminars equipping women to lead prayer groups, teach Sunday School, and serve communities.
🌍
Mission Support
Mobilising LM members to pray for and support missionaries — partnering for global outreach.
🏠
Family Strengthening
Marriage enrichment retreats, parenting seminars, and family prayer programs — building Spirit-filled homes.
