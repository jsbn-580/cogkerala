About This Department

Counselling

The Counselling Department of Church of God Kerala provides compassionate pastoral care, mental health support, and spiritual guidance to individuals and families across all 14 districts of Kerala. Rooted in Biblical truth and empowered by the Holy Spirit, our counsellors walk alongside those in need.

"Come to me, all you who are weary and burdened, and I will give you rest." — Matthew 11:28

What We Do

Our Ministries

The Counselling Department serves through these key areas of ministry across Kerala.

01
Pastoral Counselling

One-on-one spiritual guidance from trained pastoral counsellors grounded in Biblical wisdom and prayer.

02
Family Mediation

Helping families navigate conflict, restoration, and reconciliation with Christ at the centre.

03
Grief & Trauma Support

Compassionate care for those walking through loss, trauma, and life transitions.

04
Marriage Enrichment

Pre-marital and marital counselling to strengthen God-ordained unions across Kerala.

05
Youth Counselling

Age-appropriate guidance for young people facing pressures of modern life, identity, and faith.

06
Crisis Intervention

Immediate support for those in spiritual, emotional, or situational crisis — available around the clock.
