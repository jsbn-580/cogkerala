About the Board

Creating Authentic Worship Experiences

The Music Board of the Church of God (Full Gospel) in Kerala State is dedicated to fostering musical excellence and meaningful worship experiences that glorify God and edify the body of Christ.

Our seven-member board oversees all aspects of music ministry, from worship direction to training musicians and establishing musical standards across all churches. We believe that worship music is a powerful expression of faith and devotion that strengthens spiritual life.

With a commitment to both traditional and contemporary forms of worship, the Music Board works to develop worship leaders, coordinate special musical events, and ensure that every church service reflects excellence and heartfelt devotion to the Lord.
